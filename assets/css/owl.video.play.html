Custom Website Services Consists of 7 Easy Process In Order To Create Magic At Our Web Development Company

Kick-Off and Delivery

The very first and initial step of creating a custom website solution for our clients at our web design company includes a telephonic or video meeting with the client. The purpose of the meeting will be to discuss your project and document it so everything can be done according to the brief.

Right after the meeting, our experts will directly jump into the project by researching and implementing ways to develop an experience for your users that they will remember for a lifetime.

Digital Strategy

Digital Strategy plays a vital role and should be done before anything is finalized because strategizing allows us to work step by step toward the goals without missing out on any factor and that is what the best digital strategy agencies do.

After the first step of drafting the document, our experts move towards the strategy on which they are going to work and implement to make your website stand out and bring greater conversions and audience to your brand.

Information Architecture

The third and one of the most important steps of website development is gathering all the new and existing information and documenting them so none of the information or aspect is left out.

The production manager and the digital strategist at the best digital agency, Design Layer Agency, work together to build up the information for the designers and the developers who work according to their briefs.

Design Mockup

We move on to the 4th step, which is designing mockups. After the information gets your approval, it is passed on to the designers who start working on the wireframes. Wireframes are the two-dimensional design of the web page that will specify what feature will come at what place.

When the wireframe gets a thumbs up, we move on to the next phase which is creating a prototype. The prototype is a model and the realistic version of the page which is done according to the wireframe.

Coding & Development

The fifth and most important step which you all were waiting for is the coding and development of the website. Once designers are done with the design they pass up the design to the developers who start to code and develop the website in the language it was decided upon. Frontend developers at the creative web development agency will begin working on the visuals of the sites and the backend developers will work where all the magic happens. Our team of professional developers is highly skilled and can tackle any kind of challenge thrown their way and come out as the winner and that is why they will create something extraordinary for you every time.

Quality Assurance

After development and before sending it out for your approval, we at the best website development company check for all the bugs and issues that might be in the website. We pass the website to the quality assurance specialists who look through the whole design and code and check for any errors. If there are any errors, it goes back to the developers who rectify the errors, and then it is ready to go out for your approval.

Launch & Optimization

Once all the approvals and revisions are done, we move on to the last step which is launch and optimization. Once we are sure it is good to go, we make it live for the public where they can view your website and avail of your services.

You might think that this is it, but the best web development company thinks vice versa because it is time to do the optimization and check the performance and the grade of the website and make it safe and secure. Our optimization process makes sure that your website is ready for digital marketing and there will be no problems for you when Pay Per Click and Search Engine Optimization are done on the website.
